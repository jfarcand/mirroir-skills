Navigate to Settings > General > iPhone Storage and read the list of installed apps with their sizes. Uses remember to extract app names from the screen and scrolls to capture the full list.

## Steps

1. Launch **Settings**
2. Wait for "General" to appear
3. Tap "General"
4. Wait for "iPhone Storage" to appear
5. Tap "iPhone Storage"
6. Wait for "iPhone Storage" to appear
7. Remember: Read all visible app names and their storage sizes from the list.
8. Screenshot: "app_list_page1"
9. swipe: "up"
10. Remember: Read all visible app names and sizes. Note any new apps not seen on the previous screen.
11. Screenshot: "app_list_page2"
12. swipe: "up"
13. Remember: Read any remaining app names and sizes. Compile the full list from all pages.
14. Screenshot: "app_list_page3"
