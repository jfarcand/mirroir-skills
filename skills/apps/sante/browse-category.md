Open the Santé app, navigate to the Rechercher (Browse) tab, and explore a health category. The AI reads available metrics from the chosen category.

## Steps

1. Launch **Santé**
2. Wait for "Résumé" to appear
3. Tap "Rechercher"
4. Wait for "Catégories" to appear
5. Screenshot: "browse_categories"
6. Tap "Cœur"
7. Wait for "Cœur" to appear
8. Remember: Read all available heart metrics listed on this screen.
9. Screenshot: "heart_category"
10. Verify "Cœur" is visible
