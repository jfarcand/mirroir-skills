Open the Santé (Health) app and read the daily activity summary from the Résumé tab. The AI extracts calories, workout duration, distance, and step count from the summary cards.

## Steps

1. Launch **Santé**
2. Wait for "Résumé" to appear
3. Tap "Résumé"
4. Wait for "Activité" to appear
5. Remember: Read all summary card values — Activité (calories), Entraînements (duration), Distance, and Pas (steps).
6. Screenshot: "health_summary"
7. Verify "Activité" is visible
